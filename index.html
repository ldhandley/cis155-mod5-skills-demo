Harvest Moon SNES

Harvest Moon was a groundbreaking game that introduced the farming simulation genre. Released in 1996, it became a beloved classic, and the inspiration for later farming simulation games like Stardew Valley.

Super Mario World

Super Mario World, released in 1990, is one of the most iconic platform games of all time, introducing Yoshi to the Mario universe.

The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past

A Link to the Past, released in 1991, is a landmark for action-adventure games, setting a standard for the genre.
